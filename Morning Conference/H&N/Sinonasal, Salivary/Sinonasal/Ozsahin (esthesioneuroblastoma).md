# Study
- 77 patients 
	- M0
	- 1971-2004
	- Kadish stages: A 15%,B 38%,C 48%
	- R0 rate: 60%
	- Chemo: 27%
	- MFU: 6y

# Results
- 5y OS: 64
- 5y DFS: 57
- 5y LRC: 62
- 5y LC: 70
- MVI for OS, DFS, LRC, and LC: 
	- not T4
	- N0
	- not R2
	- > 54 Gy
